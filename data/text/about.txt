The Capillary Electrophoresis (CE) Toolbox is a software for computing Capillary Electrophoresis parameters. It is developped and maintained by the [ref="http://cetoolbox.github.io/"]CEToolbox Team[/ref] (University of Strasbourg / CNRS) and is based on standard functions decribed on the [ref="http://cetoolbox.github.io/"]CEToolbox website[/ref].

For more informations, visit the website: [ref="http://cetoolbox.github.io"][color=#08598E]http://cetoolbox.github.io/[/color][/ref]
